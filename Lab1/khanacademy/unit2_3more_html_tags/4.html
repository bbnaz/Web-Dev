Chocolate muffin recipe

  • Time: 25-35min
Ingredients Quantity
self-raising flour 250g
cocoa powder 25g
baking powder 2tsp
caster sugar 175g
milk 175ml
large eggs 2
mild olive oil 100ml
plain chocolate chunks 75g
vanilla extract 1-2tsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 170C. Line a 12-hole deep muffin tray with paper muffin cases.

Step 2:Sift together the flour, cocoa and baking powder. Stir in the sugar. Add the remaining ingredients and mix lightly together.

Step 3:Spoon the mixture into the prepared cases. Bake for approximately 25 mins or until golden and springy to the touch. Cool for 10 mins; transfer to a wire rack and leave to cool completely.

Banana and kiwi smoothie

  • Time: 5-10min
Ingredients Quantity
Ripe Kiwifruits 1.5
Ripe Banana 1
Milk 1/2 cup
Honey 1/2 tablespoon

Step 1:Peel and cut banana and kiwi fruits into medium pieces.

Step 2:Pour milk in the jar of a blender. Add chopped banana, kiwi and honey.

Step 3:Blend them until smooth. Kiwi banana smoothie is ready; pour it into a serving glass and serve.

Pancakes

  • Time: 20min
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 1 cup
white sugar 2 tablespoons
baking powder 2 teaspoons
salt 1 teaspoon or to taste
milk 1 cup
egg 1
vegetable oil 2 tablespoons

Step 1:Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, oil, and egg. Mix until smooth.

Step 2:Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
