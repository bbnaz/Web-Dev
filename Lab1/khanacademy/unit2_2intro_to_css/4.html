Travel to Bali

You will visit an ancient forest where sacred macaques protect mossy temples and monuments. You will see how traditional spices are grown and try Kopi Luwak. Take Instagram pictures near rice fields and swing on a swing in the middle of the jungle. You will also discover one of the most important temples of Bali and find out what is the secret of happiness of smiling Balinese.

What awaits you

  • Tegenungan Waterfall and Monkey Forest
  • Rice terraces, plantations and swings in the jungle
  • Taman-Ayun Temple, or "Beautiful Garden"
